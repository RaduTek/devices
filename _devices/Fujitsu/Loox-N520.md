### Story

I found this Pocket PC in a flea market in 2019. Before this one, I had a Mio P350, which sucked. This one however is a really nice design, built to be a PDA first and a GPS navigator second. Unfortunately I didn't get it with a charger, but I made a DIY battery holder that I hooked up to a TP4056 module to charge the battery.

Later I found an Asus A636 PDA, which coincidentally uses the same pinout for the cradle connector, and I finally had a cable to use to charge the Loox properly.
