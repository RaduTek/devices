## Origin Story

I found this unique laptop in the flea market for a very good price, along with it's original carrying case. It was working, having a charged battery. From what the dude was saying to me, it sounded like he was the original owner.

## Upgrades

I have performed the following upgrades:

- **Memory**: from the original 512 MB Micro-DIMM to a 1 GB Micro-DIMM I found on AliExpress
- **Storage**: from the original 60 GB 1.8" HDD to a 64 GB mSATA SSD in an adapter ([showcased in this video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=s-ynq5oBwmg))
- **Wireless adapter**: from the original Intel 3945ABG (802.11a/b/g) to Intel 5100 AGN mPCIe card
- **Cooling**: replaced the original thermal pads and compound, and added some extra thermal pads and thermal mass to increase thermal mass
